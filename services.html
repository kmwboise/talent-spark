Services

Executive and Professional Search

In a competitive industry and a tight labor market, Talent Spark will proactively find qualified passive talent to secure the best hires for your company and improve the quality of your candidate pools. We listen to get to know your company and culture to find candidates that will help your business thrive.

HireRamp

Need to hire a 100 people in a short timeframe? No problem! TalentSpark will build and manage overall hiring project plans including key timelines and deliverables, stakeholder management, communication, and mitigation of risk areas to keep your hiring goals on track and ensure your hiring needs are met.

Recruiting Process Outsourced (RPO)

Is your company growing but you don’t have the time or expertise to build an internal recruiting function? Recruiting Process Outsourced (RPO) provides a flexible and scalable solution to manage all of your company’s recruiting efforts and vacancies. We manage the process from end-to-end including job postings, proactive sourcing, candidate screening, developing interview guides, interview facilitation and offer negotiation to ensure you find and secure the best candidates for your business.

Heart and Hustle

People are not a just resume and your vacancy is not just a job. We have a passion for people and enjoy the human aspects of recruiting. We love to uncover what drives, motivates and inspires people and help match great humans with great opportunities. We approach our work with heart, humility and humor.

If you’re researching recruiting firms, you likely have a serious hiring need. You can count on us to approach your recruitment with a sense of speed, urgency and anticipation. We hustle to get things done and get your role filled.
