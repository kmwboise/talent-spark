TalentSpark Difference

You don’t like headhunters. We don’t either.

We respect your time and value our relationship with you. We know that as a business owner or HR Manager, your time is limited. We won't send you unqualified or unvetted resumes. Our job is to learn your business and narrow the field so you’re spending time with the right people. We leverage our Fortune 500 recruiting experience to help you find and select a candidate that will be successful in the context of your company and its culture.

With decades of recruiting experience and thousands of professional, technical and executive searches under our belt, TalentSpark has the knowledge and expertise to find top talent for all your critical hiring needs.
