TalentSpark Difference

You don’t like headhunters. We don’t either.

We respect your time and value our relationship with you. We know that as a business owner or HR Manager, recruiting is only a portion of your job and your time is limited. We don’t throw spaghetti at the wall in the form of unqualified or unvetted candidate resumes. Our job is to learn your business and narrow the field so you’re spending time with the right people. We leverage our Fortune 500 recruiting experience to help you find and select a candidate that will be successful in the context of your company.

Heart and Hustle

People are not a just resume and your vacancy is not just a job. We have a passion for people and enjoy the human aspects of recruiting. We love to uncover what drives, motivates and inspires people and help match great humans with great opportunities. We approach our work with heart, humility and humor.

If you’re researching recruiting firms, you likely have a serious hiring need. You can count on us to approach your recruitment with a sense of speed, urgency and anticipation. We hustle to get things done and get your role filled.
